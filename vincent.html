VINCENT THE ORANGE CAT

The cat of all times. Found in the trash. Just a little guy.

His Orange Majesty displays non-newtonian fluid properties.

He likes sitting in crinkly plastic bags.

Vincent also likes to lounge around that old wooden chair, although he is yet to figure out (or purposefully ignores) the correct way of sitting on said piece of furniture.
